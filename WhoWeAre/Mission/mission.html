Our Mission

At Pathway to Possibilities, our mission is to empower women and underserved youth to explore, pursue, and excel in STEM fields. We strive to bridge the opportunity gap by providing access to mentorship, education, and resources that inspire the next generation of innovators. Our Founder

Hi! I'm Katelynn Blank and I'm currently finishing my degree in Microelectronic Engineering at the Rochester Institute of Technology. Inspired by a beloved mentor who passed away this year, I've founded the Pathways to Possibilities Foundation to empower women and underprivileged youth in STEM. My own journey navigating the challenges of being a woman in STEM has driven me to create opportunities for others. At the Pathways to Possibilities Foundation, we're dedicated to fostering a supportive community where everyone can explore, learn, and thrive in STEM fields.
